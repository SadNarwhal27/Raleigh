**d20 Based Commands:**
*Roll a d20* - $roll
*Add a Modifier* - $roll +5
*Roll with Advantage/Disadvantage* - $roll advantage or $roll disadvantage

**Other Dice Commands:**
*Roll Percentile Dice* - $roll percentile
*Roll a Series of Dice* - $roll 4d6
*Add a Modifier* - $roll 4d6 +5

**Utility Commands:**
*Get a List of Commands* - $roll help
*Turn a Roll into a PM* - $roll 4d6 +5 secret
